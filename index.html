How to Play

1 · The SetupEveryone sees a topic and 16 words. All players except the Chameleon also see the secret word.
2 · Give a ClueEach player says one word that hints at the secret word — without making it too obvious. The Chameleon must bluff.
3 · VoteAfter all clues, vote on who you think the Chameleon is. If correct, Chameleon can still win by guessing the word!
